# Sakshi-E-Commerce-Sales-Dashboard
This Dashboard is  created to analyze Sakshi e-commerce sales data across multiple states, including Texas, New York, Illinois, Florida, and California. This project was a deep dive into understanding sales performance, customer behavior, and profitability trends.

⭐ Project Overview :
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of e-commerce sales data, including:
✅Sales Performance: Total sales, profit, and average order value (AOV) by state and quarter.
✅Customer Insights: Sales distribution by customer name and payment mode.
✅Product Trends: Quantity sold by category and subcategory, highlighting top-performing products.
✅Profit Analysis: Monthly profit trends and state-wise profitability.

📊This dashboard empowers businesses to:
- Identify high-performing states and categories.
- Optimize inventory and marketing strategies based on customer preferences.
- Track profitability trends and make data-driven decisions.

💡Key Insights :
Here are some valuable insights uncovered through this dashboard:

➡️Furniture & Books Top Categories: Furniture and books were the most popular categories across all states, with books accounting for 33.53% of sales in California.
➡️Payment Preferences: Online payments and credit cards were the most preferred payment modes, especially in Texas and Florida.
➡️Seasonal Trends: Sales peaked in July and August, indicating a strong back-to-school shopping season.

Steps I Followed :
- Data Collection: Gathered e-commerce sales data from multiple states, including sales, profit, quantity, and customer details.
- Data Cleaning: Removed duplicates, handled missing values, and standardized data formats for consistency.
- Data Modeling: Created relationships between tables (e.g., sales, customers, products) to enable seamless analysis.
- Visualization: Designed interactive charts, maps, and tables to highlight key metrics and trends.
- Testing & Optimization: Tested the dashboard for accuracy and optimized it for performance and user-friendliness.
